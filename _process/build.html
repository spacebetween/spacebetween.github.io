Agile

We've spent a lot of time optimising our processes, we've found that Agile methodologies are our preferred project management style.

This means that we work in sprints, you've probably already read about our Design Sprints. We use a similar idea, but a different form of sprint in our build stage. This means that unlike traditional methodologies we can be building while we design, test, and ultimately deploy.

This means we have a shorter feedback loop meaning a faster delivery and, more importantly, a much better solution.

Modern Technology

At Space Between we push the envelope when it comes to modern technology and we're very proud of the fact we get excited about the latest piece of tech.

We are all tech enthusiasts here, which means in our spare time we're constantly playing with new bits of kit.

But what does this give you? It's that very love for tech that means we are in the best position to pick the right technology for your solution. We take into account the type of website, the amount of security necessary, the volume of users, the type of product and a load of other variables to come up with what's perfect for you.

Code Quality

Just like Steve Jobs cared about the look of the circuit boards that nobody could see inside the Apple computers, we care about the code that nobody will ever see.

This means we adhere to rules when developing our websites. We have a strict list of coding guidelines that dictate how we write code, we then use code reviews to make sure they've been adhered to. For more challenging tasks we even use a technique called pair programming, this means we get two developers to code one task. Well, two heads are better than one.

We want to involve you

We think collaboration is the key to success, working together means we have a much higher chance of coming up with the best solution, after all, you know your product better than we ever could. We invite you to be as involved in the process as possible. You can attend daily 5 minutes conference calls about your project to hear how things are going.

We've got nothing to hide, we are completely transparent, you'll be able to hear us discussing your product, you'll hear our ideas, you'll be able to get involved with the best ways to solve them. These conversations happen regardless, but unlike other companies, we invite you to them.
