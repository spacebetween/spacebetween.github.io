User testing

The most important group of people on the internet is without a doubt your users.

The first thing to remember is who your intended audience is, your users. What they think about your website is the single most important thing. That is why at Space Between we use actual User Testing wherever possible.

Just because nobody complains doesnâ€™t mean all parachutes are perfect. Jacob at UX Booth

It's very hard to put yourself in your user's shoes, this is because you know so much about your product and website. You know exactly how it should be used, this is why the best way to learn what your users think, is to speak to them.

We use a variety of techniques to get your users' feedback, from asking strangers in the street to try out prototypes, to getting people online to try your product. But the best way to test your product is to invite your actual users and customers to get their opinions. That way we can make exactly what they want and, ultimately, something they will love.

Conversion Rate Optimisation (CRO)

One of the most common ways of adding real value to your business is through Conversion Rate Optimisation (CRO for short). The goal of CRO is to increase the value of your website, by increasing the percentage of visitors performing your website's goal.

A few examples of areas we've increased:

  • Basket value of an e-commerce website.
  • The conversion rate of a booking flow.
  • Website registrations.
  • Sign ups to an email database.
  • Posts read on a blog.
  • Time spent on a website.

Because CRO is a very personal experience, have a chat with us to find out how it can benefit you.
