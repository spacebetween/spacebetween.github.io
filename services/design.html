App design

UX

User experience is all about the people who use your site the most - your users. A great experience is about more than just the way something looks; it’s about having impact, and value, and creating a pleasurable moment in time. Through tracking, user research and diving deep into your coding, we can create the best experience for your customers.

User research

Every business is different - and so are their customers. That’s why user research is a big part of our design process at Space Between. It helps us to understand what makes your customers tick, what gets them going, and what’s going to make them click the little red button in the top corner of the screen. And no one wants that, do they?
